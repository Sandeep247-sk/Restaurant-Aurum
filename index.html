Our Story

Crafted With Passion,
Served With Pride

Aurum was born from a singular belief — that dining is not merely sustenance, but an art form. Since 2009, we have welcomed guests into a world of considered flavour, where each plate is a conversation between ingredient, technique, and season.

Our head chef, Marco Delacroix, trained under three Michelin-starred mentors across Paris, Tokyo, and New York before returning to redefine what fine dining means in this city.

We source exclusively from heritage farms, coastal fishing villages, and artisan producers who share our commitment to quality without compromise.
